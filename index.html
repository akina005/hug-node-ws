How You Can Help

You don't need to be a scientist to make a difference. Small actions lead to big changes.

Reduce Single-Use Plastics

Bring your own bag, bottle, and straw. Refuse disposable plastics whenever possible.

Choose Sustainable Seafood

Check labels and buy seafood that is certified as sustainably sourced.

Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

Walk, bike, or use public transport. The ocean absorbs heat, so less CO2 helps the ocean cool down.

Participate in Cleanups

Join a local beach cleanup or simply pick up trash when you visit the coast.
